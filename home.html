Welcome to Foodology

We offer an affordable, sustainable farm-to-table solution for an undernourished society. We are proud of ourselves for the quality and outstanding standards of the food we place on your tables. We started this vision as a produce Coop and slowly developed a family company rich in flavor and values.

We guarantee our customers that the food they get here is non-GMO, organic, local, and sustainable. Each plate is a creation per se because there is much thought in them. We balance minerals, vitamins, nutrients, and fibers to elaborate dishes with the right daily healthy proportions.

Families run our business. We gather people with extraordinary talents to achieve the highest standards without elevated prices. We also give guidance and classes to help our community expand its knowledge of Victory and urban gardens. These classes are free, and people must avail themselves of the seeds, soil, and growing boxes. It is fantastic to see little children harvesting their food at such an early age. This keeps the traditions solid and durable.

Our company model is eclectic and full of holistic pathways to enrich our products and create a solid internal atmosphere. We believe what we preach, and everyone in our company cherishes the same values and principles, which guarantee the fulfillment of our purpose: Support the coming generations through healthy, whole foods and practices.
